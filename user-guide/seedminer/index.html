Required Reading

To install boot9strap on your device, we derive your device's unique encryption key.

To accomplish this, we use a tool called Seedminer to calculate the DSiWare encryption key (movable.sed) for your device. We then use this DSiWare encryption key to decrypt a compatible DSiWare application and inject an exploit into it to run our own code (the exact exploit used will depend on your device's region).

If this key were completely random, the encryption key would be impossible to break on current computing hardware. Fortunately, the first half of the key (movable_part1.sed) is actually mathematically related to your device's Friend Code, allowing us to break the encryption. For information on how Seedminer works, please see this presentation.

This method uses a powerful graphics card to perform the calculations needed. A volunteer-run website is used for the purpose of assisting you with this method.

Instructions

Section I - Prep Work

  1. Insert your SD card into your computer
  2. Navigate to the Nintendo 3DS folder on your SD card
  3. Copy the 32 character long name of the folder you see inside Nintendo 3DS
  4. Do not go inside any more folders
  5. If you see multiple folders with long, random names inside Nintendo 3DS without opening any other folders, perform the following:
  6. Rename the Nintendo 3DS folder to BACKUP_Nintendo 3DS
  7. Reinsert your SD card into your device and power on your device
  8. Wait for the device to generate the SD card data
  9. Power off your device and reinsert your SD card into your computer
  10. Navigate to the Nintendo 3DS folder on your SD card
  11. Copy the 32 character long name of the folder you see
  12. Delete the Nintendo 3DS folder
  13. Rename the BACKUP_Nintendo 3DS folder to Nintendo 3DS
  14. Paste your 32 character long folder name into a document you can reference later
  15. This folder name is also known as your "ID0"
  16. Power on your device
  17. Go to your Friend List (the orange Face Icon in the top row of your Home Menu)
  18. If you receive an error and are kicked out of the menu, you either must create a new Mii or your device cannot connect to Nintendo's servers (due to a ban or connection issues)
  19. Find your Mii profile, then find the "Friend Code" field on the top screen

Section II - Seedminer

  1. Open Bruteforce Movable on your computer
  2. This site can automate the retrieval of movable_part1.sed (using a bot 3DS console) and the breaking of your device's DSiWare encryption (using a volunteer's computer to run Seedminer)
  3. If the site isn't working, join Nintendo Homebrew on Discord and ask (in English) for someone there to assist you
  4. Enter your device's Friend Code (with no spaces or dashes) into the "Your friend code" field
  5. Paste your device's 32 character long folder name into the "Your ID0" field
  6. Do not attempt to enter the ID0 by hand. Ensure the ID0 is entered correctly by copying and pasting it from where you saved it in the previous section
  7. Select "Go"
  8. If the site immediately goes to step 4, download your movable.sed file and continue with the next section
  9. When prompted, use the "Register Friend" button on your device to add the friend code of the bot 3DS console
  10. Wait for the site to update
  11. If it does not, wait a few minutes before refreshing the page once
  12. Once the site processes your information, you will be given the option to "Continue" or "Do another device". Click the green "Continue" button
  13. Wait for the remainder of the process to complete
  14. This is usually fast (1-5 minutes) but in some cases, can take up to half an hour
  15. During this process, the bot you added may not show up on your 3DS. As long as the website updates, this is not an issue
  16. If you are still waiting after half an hour, join Nintendo Homebrew on Discord and ask (in English) for someone there to assist you
  17. When the process has completed, download your movable.sed file from the site
  18. Keep this file as you will need it in the upcoming pages

This method of using Seedminer for further exploitation uses your movable.sed file to create a custom DSiWare backup that exploits the system to dump system DSiWare.

This route is only recommended if you are for some reason unable to follow the PicHaxx + universal-otherapp route.
